HerPhase — Pre-Publish Audit

Here's what I found across the codebase. Before we decide what to fix, I have a few questions for you.

🚨 Blockers (must fix before launch)
No Privacy Policy or Terms of Service

HerPhase collects menstrual cycle data, health metrics, and mood logs. This is sensitive health data. Publishing without a privacy policy is a legal risk in most regions (GDPR, US state laws).

Dev artifacts exposed in /public

14 design exploration HTML files (design-exploration-*.html, mockup.html, etc.) are publicly accessible at yoursite.com/design-exploration-v5.html. Also: test_output.js, verify_spec.js in project root.

Sentry example route accessible

/sentry-example-page is a live route that exists purely for Sentry testing. Should be removed before going live.

⚠️ Should fix (important for quality)
Hardcoded "Good morning" greeting

Dashboard always says "Good morning" regardless of time of day. Should be time-aware (morning / afternoon / evening).

No 404 page

Next.js will show a generic 404. A branded not-found page would be more polished.

IS_PRO_FEATURE flag is false (PRs page)

There's a commented-out paywall gate in prs/page.tsx. A monetisation decision needs to be made before launch.

PWA icon uses "any maskable" for both purposes

The manifest sets purpose: "any maskable" for both icons. Best practice is to separate purpose: "any" and purpose: "maskable" into separate entries for better home screen display.

Theme hardcoded to "rose" — no dark mode

Layout.tsx forces data-theme="rose". No dark mode support. For a health app used at night (checking cycle, logging mood), dark mode matters.
